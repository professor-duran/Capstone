\textbf{How to use this guide:} This document is your sprint-by-sprint companion for Capstone Design. It is \emph{not} a replacement for the Course Syllabus, the Course Text, or Canvas; it is a navigation aid that tells you where to focus your energy each sprint. For each sprint, start by reading that sprint's section and pay special attention to any box labeled ``Common Pitfall'' or ``Pro Tip'' (those address the most frequent issues). Read the relevant section at the \emph{start} of each sprint, not the end.

\textbf{Three documents work together}: (1)~this Student Guide (sprint-level navigation), (2)~the Course Text (deep reference, 22 chapters across 5 parts), and (3)~Canvas (assignments, rubrics, deadlines). The Course Syllabus is the authoritative source for grading and policies.
