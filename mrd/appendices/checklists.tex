\section*{Engineering Calculation Package Checklist}
This checklist collates all requirements for the Engineering Calculation Package from Chapters~6, 9, and 11. Verify completeness before CDR submission.

\textbf{For every calculation or analysis in the package:} \\
$\square$ Purpose stated: which requirement (by ID) or design question is being addressed \\
$\square$ Assumptions stated explicitly (values, sources, limitations) \\
$\square$ Methodology documented: equations with textbook or standard references \\
$\square$ For simulations: mesh settings, boundary conditions, convergence criteria documented \\
$\square$ Results presented with appropriate units and significant figures \\
$\square$ Plots, contour maps, or time-domain responses included where applicable \\
$\square$ Conclusion stated: does the result meet the requirement? If not, what action is recommended? \\
$\square$ Source citations for all referenced data, equations, and material properties

\textbf{Package-level completeness:} \\
$\square$ All ``Must Have'' requirements have at least one supporting analysis \\
$\square$ Structural analysis (loads, stress, safety factors) for load-bearing components \\
$\square$ Thermal analysis (if applicable): heat generation, dissipation, operating temperature \\
$\square$ Electrical analysis (if applicable): power budget, voltage regulation, current draw \\
$\square$ Control/software analysis (if applicable): response time, stability, state machines \\
$\square$ Fluid analysis (if applicable): flow rates, pressure drop, pump sizing \\
$\square$ Hand calculations cross-checked against simulation where both are feasible \\
$\square$ Independent review: at least one team member other than the author reviewed each calculation \\
$\square$ Package is organized as a narrative document, not a file dump of screenshots \\
$\square$ Table of contents or index links each analysis to its corresponding requirement
