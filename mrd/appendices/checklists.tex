\chapter{Quick Reference Checklists}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Appendix C: Quick Reference Checklists}

\section*{Sprint~0 Kickoff Checklist}
$\square$ Team formed, roles assigned (Scrum Master, CFO, Comm Lead, Tech Lead) \\
$\square$ Team repository created (Teams group, shared file structure) \\
$\square$ Client contact information confirmed \\
$\square$ Kickoff meeting prepared (agenda, discovery questions, slide deck) \\
$\square$ Kickoff meeting conducted \\
$\square$ Follow-up email sent within 24 hours \\
$\square$ EHS Safety Training completed (Canvas module) \\
$\square$ Individual Design Log started

\section*{SOW Submission Checklist}
$\square$ Project title and team information \\
$\square$ Track classification (Build / Paper / Competition) with justification \\
$\square$ Five-Element Problem Statement \\
$\square$ In-Scope and Out-of-Scope sections \\
$\square$ Deliverables list (SDI and SDII) \\
$\square$ Schedule with milestones and Gantt chart \\
$\square$ Budget estimate with contingency \\
$\square$ Team roles and responsibilities \\
$\square$ Assumptions and constraints \\
$\square$ Risk summary (top 3--5 risks) \\
$\square$ PA reviewed and approved \\
$\square$ Client reviewed and approved

\section*{PDR Readiness Checklist}
$\square$ Complete SDRD (all requirements with IDs, SHALL/SHOULD, TAID, MoSCoW) \\
$\square$ $\geq$3 distinct concepts generated with graphics \\
$\square$ Pugh screening and weighted decision matrix with sensitivity analysis \\
$\square$ Proof-of-concept evidence (data, not just claims) \\
$\square$ Sustainability assessment with CLO~5 tool applied \\
$\square$ Engineering standards identified \\
$\square$ Risk register with mitigations \\
$\square$ VCRM plan (all entries Open with TAID assigned) \\
$\square$ PDR Report reviewed by team \\
$\square$ Mock PDR conducted $\geq$3 days before formal review \\
$\square$ PDR Report sent to client \\
$\square$ Presentation rehearsed, timed, within allotted time

\section*{CDR Readiness Checklist}
$\square$ Detailed design complete (CAD, wiring, software architecture) \\
$\square$ Engineering Calculation Package (all critical analyses) \\
$\square$ BOM complete and validated (prices, availability, lead times verified) \\
$\square$ Critical components ordered or received \\
$\square$ InnoHub Manufacturing Review Meeting completed \\
$\square$ Hazard Assessment and Safety Plan approved by PA \\
$\square$ FMEA updated with design mitigations \\
$\square$ VCRM updated (analysis verifications complete) \\
$\square$ CDR Report reviewed by team \\
$\square$ CDR Report sent to client \\
$\square$ Presentation rehearsed

\section*{FDR Readiness Checklist}
$\square$ All V\&V testing complete \\
$\square$ VCRM: all requirements dispositioned (Verified / Failed / Waived / Deferred) \\
$\square$ As-designed vs. as-built comparison documented \\
$\square$ O\&M manual written \\
$\square$ Lessons learned section complete \\
$\square$ Future work recommendations documented \\
$\square$ FDR Report reviewed by team \\
$\square$ FDR Report sent to client \\
$\square$ Showcase poster submitted for printing \\
$\square$ Demo tested in Showcase space \\
$\square$ Digital Design Archive organized \\
$\square$ All final deliverables in progress

\section*{Final Checkout Checklist}
$\square$ FDR presented \\
$\square$ Showcase attended \\
$\square$ Digital Design Archive submitted \\
$\square$ ``What I Wish I Would Have Known'' video submitted \\
$\square$ Project Synopsis submitted \\
$\square$ Next Steps Email (post-FDR) sent to client \\
$\square$ Project Goal Attainment submitted \\
$\square$ Feedback Loop \#3 completed \\
$\square$ Individual Performance Evaluation \#2 completed \\
$\square$ Final Course Survey completed \\
$\square$ All borrowed equipment returned \\
$\square$ Workspace cleaned and locker emptied \\
$\square$ Budget Tracker final version uploaded \\
$\square$ Checkout meeting with PA completed
